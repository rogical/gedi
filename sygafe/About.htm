About SYGAFE V2

 

SYGAFE, (SYstème de Génération d’un cAtalogue de Fiches de rEcette) is a tool  capable to create acceptance books for VND and MND.

 

It uses XML  documents.

This project replaces the previous tool RECETTE existent on the VM system.

It offers the same functionalities.

 

SYGAFE was developed by Jacob NGUYEN, trainee at the service INDUS/DSIS in ALCATEL CIT LANNION and upgraded at the CTR TIMISOARA, and updating at the OSD/OPS/SDM in CIT ALCATEL VELIZY.

 

The first version was delivered the 20 juin 2002.

The 2.03 version was delivered on march 2004.

The 2.04 version was delivered on december 2004.

The 2.05 version was delivered on novembre 2006.

The 2.06 version was delivered on novembre 2009.
